Notable Works of B.B. Warfield

The Inspiration and Authority of the Bible

Warfield’s definitive defense of biblical inerrancy and the doctrine that Scripture, in its original autographs, is without error. Often found in collected essays and reprints.

Counterfeit Miracles

A historical critique of modern miracle claims arguing for cessationism — that certain extraordinary gifts ceased after the apostolic age.

Studies in Tertullian and Augustine

Historical and doctrinal studies tracing how key Latin theologians shaped Christian doctrine, especially on grace and the church.

The Plan of Salvation (lectures)

Lectures and notes outlining Warfield's systematic theology on decrees, grace, and redemption.

Collected Essays & Reviews

Warfield wrote hundreds of reviews and essays on theology, history, and contemporary issues — an excellent way to see his argument style and scholarly range.
